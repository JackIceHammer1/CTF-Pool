In search of spells, I tread the ancient halls,
Beneath the moon's dim glow, where darkness calls.
Alas, no incantation yet I've found,
To match the strength with which my will is bound.

The Alabaster Lord, with wisdom deep,
His magic's secrets in his chambers keep.
Yet even he, with all his mystic lore,
Lacks a spell that can my power restore.

I stand alone, amidst the shadows drear,
Yearning for magic that will draw near.
But until that day, I'll wield my might,
And brave the darkness with relentless fight.
